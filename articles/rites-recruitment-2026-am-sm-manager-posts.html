Selection Process

Process Overview

RITES selection process follows weighted evaluation:

  1. Written Examination: 60% weightage - Technical and General Knowledge
  2. Interview: 40% weightage - Personal interview
  3. Document Verification: Final verification of certificates

Evaluation Criteria

  • Technical knowledge relevant to the post
  • Work experience and professional achievements
  • Communication and interpersonal skills
  • Managerial and leadership capabilities
  • Adaptability to RITES work culture

How to Apply Online

Step-by-Step Application Process:

  1. Visit Official Website: www.rites.com
  2. Navigate to Careers: Click "Career" section in the main menu
  3. Select Position: Choose the desired post
  4. Read Notification: Download and read the official notification
  5. Click "Apply Online": Proceed to online application portal
  6. Register: Create account using valid email and mobile number
  7. Fill Application: Enter personal, educational, and experience details
  8. Upload Documents: Scan and upload required certificates
  9. Submit: Review and submit before the deadline
  10. Print Acknowledgment: Save/print confirmation

For Deputation Posts:

  • Submit application through proper channel with NOC
  • Include Vigilance clearance certificate
  • Provide DAR clearance

Documents Required

  • Essential Educational Qualification Certificates
  • Experience Certificates and Relieving Letters
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport)
  • Date of Birth Certificate
  • Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Signature

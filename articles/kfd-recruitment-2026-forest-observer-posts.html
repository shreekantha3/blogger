Selection Process

The recruitment selection process follows a multi-stage approach:

Step 1: SSLC Merit-Based Shortlisting

  • Initial shortlisting based on SSLC marks
  • No written examination involved
  • Candidates called for next stages based on academic merit

Step 2: Physical Standard Test (PST)

  • Male Candidates: Height minimum 163 cm, Chest 79 cm with 5 cm expansion
  • Female Candidates: Height minimum 150 cm, Weight 40 kg minimum
  • ST Candidates: Relaxation in physical standards applicable

Step 3: Physical Endurance Test (PET)

  • Race: 1500m (male) / 800m (female)
  • Long jump and high jump events
  • Physical fitness verification

Step 4: Medical Examination

  • Medical fitness test as per forest department standards
  • Certificate from designated medical practitioner

Step 5: Document Verification

  • Verification of all original documents
  • SSLC certificate and age proof validation

Selection Process

The recruitment selection process follows a multi-stage approach:

Step 1: Application Submission

  • Online application through jobsksrlps.karnataka.gov.in
  • Upload required documents and experience certificates

Step 2: Document Verification

  • Verify educational qualifications and experience
  • Check eligibility for each position

Step 3: Interview

  • Personal interview for shortlisted candidates
  • Technical and suitability assessment

Step 4: Final Selection

  • Merit list based on qualifications and interview performance
  • Appointment order issuance

How to Apply Online

Step-by-Step Application Process:

  1. Visit Official Website: jobsksrlps.karnataka.gov.in
  2. Click "Apply Now": Register as new candidate
  3. Download Notification: Read complete advertisement carefully
  4. Check Eligibility: Verify educational and experience requirements
  5. Gather Documents:
  • Essential Qualification Degree Certificate and Mark Sheets
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
  • Date of Birth Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Signature
  • Experience Certificates (from previous employers)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any application fee?

No, there is no application fee for any category. This recruitment has full fee exemption.

What is the required experience for District Program Manager?

Candidates must have Post Graduate degree plus 5 years experience in Social Sciences, Rural Development, MSW, MBA, or Agricultural Sciences.

Is knowledge of Kannada and English mandatory?

Yes, reading, writing, and speaking skills in both Kannada and English are mandatory for these positions.

Can I apply for multiple posts?

Check the official notification. Generally, you should apply for positions matching your qualifications and experience.

Is this recruitment still ongoing?

The application deadline was July 15, 2026. Check the official website for any extended deadline or next recruitment cycle.

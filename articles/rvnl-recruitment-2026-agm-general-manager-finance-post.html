Detailed Eligibility Criteria by Level

For General Manager (Finance)

  • Government Officers: Holding Level-14 (CDA) / IDA E-8 or Level-13 with minimum 17 years Group 'A' service
  • PSU Officers: Working in IDA E-7 grade (₹1,00,000-2,60,000) with at least 4 years experience in the grade

For Additional General Manager (Finance)

  • Government Officers: Holding Level-13-A (CDA) / IDA E-7 or Level-13 with 12-14 years Group 'A' service
  • PSU Officers: Working in IDA E-6 grade (₹90,000-2,40,000) with minimum 4 years experience in the grade

For AGM Level

  • Government Officers: Holding Level-13 analogous grade OR Level-13 with 12+ years Group A service
  • PSU Officers: Working in IDA E-6 grade (₹90,000-2,40,000) with 4 years service

Age Criteria

  • GM (Finance): Below 58 years (deputation basis) or below 57 years (absorption)
  • AGM (Finance): Below 56 years
  • Calculation: Age is calculated as of the closing date of applications

How to Apply

Step-by-Step Application Process:

  1. Download Application Form: Visit rvnl.org and download the prescribed proforma from the Career/Jobs section
  2. Fill Application: Complete personal data, educational qualifications, experience details, and APAR ratings for last 5 years
  3. Get NOC: Obtain No Objection Certificate from parent department/organization
  4. Prepare Required Documents:
  • Duly filled and signed prescribed proforma (PDF format)
  • Vigilance/DAR clearance certificate
  • APAR grading for last 5 years
  • APARs (Annual Performance Appraisal Reports) for last five years
  • Service records and experience details
  • Recent passport size photograph
  1. Submit Application: Send scanned PDF copy to rvnl.deputation@rvnl.org

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this post open to fresh graduates?

No. This is a deputation post open only to serving IRAS or CPSE officers who already hold an equivalent grade. Fresh graduates cannot apply.

What is the salary for AGM/GM Finance at RVNL?

AGM (Finance) draws ₹90,000-2,40,000 IDA pay plus deputation allowance. GM (Finance) draws ₹1,00,000-2,60,000 IDA. You receive parent pay plus deputation allowance.

What are the educational qualifications required?

The candidate must be from IRAS (Indian Railway Accounts Service) or Finance & Accounts Department of CPSEs. Specific educational qualifications are as per parent service rules.

How many vacancies are available?

There is 1 vacancy for AGM/GM (Finance) position at RVNL Corporate Office locations.

What is the maximum age limit?

GM (Finance) - below 58 years (deputation) or 57 years (absorption). AGM (Finance) - below 56 years. Age is calculated as of closing date.

Is there a written examination?

No written examination. Selection is based on document screening including vigilance/DAR clearance and APAR ratings.

Can I apply directly without forwarding through my department?

No. Application must be forwarded through proper channel from your controlling officer/parent department. No direct applications will be considered.

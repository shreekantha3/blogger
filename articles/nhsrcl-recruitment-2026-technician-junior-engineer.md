<h2>Selection Process</h2>

<h3>Process Overview</h3>
The selection process includes:

1. **Computer Based Test (CBT)**
   - Online examination conducted by NHSRCL
   - Duration: 2 hours
   - Subjects: Reasoning, Numerical Ability, General Knowledge, Technical

2. **Document Verification**
   - Verification of educational and experiential certificates
   - Age and category certificate validation

3. **Medical Examination**
   - Physical fitness assessment
   - Medical certificate issuance

Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Post Minimum Qualification
All Teaching Posts Bachelor's Degree + B.Ed. in respective methods/subjects; For Language Teachers: BA with language as elective/optional subject + B.Ed.

Mandatory Requirements

  • KTET/CTET Certificate: Qualified in Teacher Eligibility Test (Paper I for Primary, Paper II for Secondary)
  • Language Knowledge: For Language Teachers - Must have studied Kannada/Hindi/English as language in SSLC/PUC/Graduation OR passed equivalent certificate exams
  • Working Knowledge: Reading, writing, and speaking skills in both Kannada and English

Age Limits (as of Recruitment Date)

  • General Merit (GM): 18-45 years
  • Category 2A/2B/3A/3B: 18-48 years
  • SC/ST/Cat-1: 18-50 years
  • Ex-Servicemen/PwD/Widows/Government Employees: Additional relaxation up to 10 years

Selection Process

The recruitment selection process follows a multi-stage approach:

Step 1: Compulsory Kannada Language Test

  • Qualifying test (150 marks) for candidates without language exemption
  • Minimum 50 marks required (not counted for merit)

Step 2: Competitive Written Examination

  • Paper 1: General Studies (100 marks) - 2 hours
  • Paper 2: Subject-Specific (100 marks) - 2 hours
  • Total Duration: 4 hours
  • 0.25 negative marking for incorrect answers

Step 3: Qualifying Marks Cutoff

  • General Category: 35% minimum qualifying marks
  • SC/ST/OBC: 33% minimum qualifying marks

Step 4: Document Verification

  • Final verification of educational qualifications
  • KTET/CTET certificate validation

Step 5: Final Merit List Preparation

  • Merit list prepared based on written exam performance
  • Separate reservation category lists prepared

Frequently Asked Questions

Is KTET/CTET compulsory for application?

Yes, KTET/CTET qualification is mandatory - Paper I for Primary level and Paper II for Secondary level teachers.

Is Kannada language knowledge mandatory?

Yes, for Language Teachers, Kannada/Hindi/English must have been studied as language in SSLC/PUC/Graduation OR passed equivalent certificate exams.

Is there negative marking in the exam?

Yes, there is 0.25 negative marking for each wrong answer in both papers.

Is there an interview process?

No, selection is completely merit-based through written examination only. No interview process.

Can I apply for multiple teaching posts?

Yes, you can apply for multiple posts with an additional fee of ₹100 per post.

Indian Army has announced Short Service Commission (Technical) recruitment for 2026. 350 SSC(T)-68 Men positions are available for Technical Graduate Course. This is an excellent opportunity for engineering graduates to serve the nation with a unique short service commission.

Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Post Minimum Qualification
SSC(T)-68 Men (Technical) Engineering Degree (B.E./B.Tech) from recognized university

Age Limits (as of Recruitment Date)

  • Minimum Age: 20 years (as on date of commencement of course)
  • Maximum Age: 27 years (as on date of commencement of course)
  • Age Relaxation:
  • SC/ST: 3 years relaxation
  • OBC: 3 years relaxation

Technical Stream Requirements

  • Eligible Streams: Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication, Computer Science & Engineering, Aeronautical Engineering, Automobile Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Metallurgical Engineering
  • Minimum Percentage: 60% or equivalent in aggregate of all semesters
  • Gender: Male candidates only
  • Marital Status: Unmarried/Widower/Divorcee without spouse

Selection Process

The recruitment selection process follows a multi-stage approach:

Step 1: Online Application

  • Register and fill application form at joinindianarmy.nic.in
  • Upload required documents and certificates

Step 2: SSB Interview

  • Two-stage Selection Process (Stage I and Stage II)
  • Interview includes intelligence test, PPDT, TAT, GDT, SD
  • Conference evaluation for final recommendation

Step 3: Document Verification

  • Original certificates and mark sheets verification
  • Eligibility and age proof verification

Step 4: Medical Examination

  • Detailed medical examination at designated military hospital
  • Fitness standards as per Indian Army guidelines

Step 5: Training

  • 10+2 months training at Officers Training Academy (OTA), Chennai
  • Successful candidates commissioned as Lieutenants

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a permanent commission?

No, this is a Short Service Commission (SSC) for 10 years, extendable up to 14 years based on service requirements.

Can women apply for SSC(T) posts?

This particular recruitment is for Men candidates only. Women candidates should check other SSC entries.

Is there any application fee?

No, there is no application fee for Indian Army officer entries.

What is the training period?

The training period is 10+2 months at Officers Training Academy (OTA), Chennai.

What is the minimum percentage required?

Candidates must have secured minimum 60% marks or equivalent in aggregate of all semesters in Engineering.

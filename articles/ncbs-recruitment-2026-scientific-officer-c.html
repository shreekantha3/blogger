Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Essential Qualifications:

  • Degree: Full Time B.V.Sc. (Bachelor of Veterinary Science) from a recognized university/institute
  • Minimum Marks: 60% aggregate in B.V.Sc. degree
  • Additional Skills: Proficient in using personal computers and its applications

Desirable Qualifications:

  • M.V.Sc. in Veterinary Pathology (preferred)
  • Work experience in management of high barrier animal facility
  • Technical skills in Health and Genetic monitoring of laboratory mice and rats
  • Knowledge of animal welfare regulatory (CCSEA) guidelines

Age Limit

  • Maximum Age: 31 years as on January 1, 2026
  • Relaxation: Includes OBC relaxation as applicable
  • Note: Candidates above 31 years are not eligible even with OBC certificate

Experience Requirements

  • Minimum Experience: 2 years' experience in relevant veterinary field (preferred)
  • Key Areas: Animal care, laboratory animal management, pathology

The monthly compensation is ₹56,100/- basic pay along with allowances as per. Central Government norms. The total package includes DA, HRA, and other benefits.

The selection involves written test/skill test/job-oriented practical test followed by interview.. Exact dates and details will be intimated to candidates in advance.

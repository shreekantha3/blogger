Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Post Minimum Qualification
Special Reserve Police Constable Passed 2nd PUC (Class 12) or equivalent qualification recognized by Govt of Karnataka (CBSE/ISC 12th, NIOS, 3-year Diploma, 2-year ITI courses)

Age Limits (as of Recruitment Date)

  • General Merit (GM): 18-33 years
  • SC/ST/Cat-1/2A/2B/3A/3B: 18-35 years
  • Tribal Candidates (Karnataka Forest Areas): 18-38 years
  • Ex-Servicemen: Service period + 3 years additional relaxation

Physical Standards (For Constable Posts)

  • Height (Male): Minimum 165 cm for General Category
  • Height (Female): Minimum 150 cm for General Category
  • Chest (Male): Minimum 85 cm (with expansion 90 cm)
  • Weight: As per medical standards

Selection Process

The recruitment selection process follows a multi-stage approach:

Step 1: Offline Written Examination

  • Written test conducted by Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA)
  • Objective type questions covering General Knowledge, Reasoning, and Professional Knowledge
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Total Marks: 100 (1 mark each, 0.25 negative marking)

Step 2: Physical Efficiency Test (PET)

  • 1600m race for males, 800m for females
  • Long jump, High jump
  • Shuttle run

Step 3: Physical Standards Test (PST)

  • Verification of physical measurements
  • Medical fitness examination

Step 4: Kannada Language Test

  • For candidates without language exemption
  • Reading, writing, and speaking skills assessment

Step 5: Document Verification

  • Verification of all original documents
  • Certificate validation

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there negative marking in the written exam?

Yes, there is 0.25 negative marking for each wrong answer in the written examination.

What is the application fee?

General Merit & OBC candidates: ₹750. SC/ST/Cat-1/Ex-Servicemen: ₹500.

Is Kannada language test mandatory?

It depends on your educational background. Candidates who haven't studied Kannada in SSLC/PUC/Graduation may need to take the Kannada Language Test.

Can I apply for multiple cadres?

Check the official notification. Typically you can apply for one cadre at a time.

What is the salary for KSRP Constable?

The pay scale is ₹37,500 - ₹76,100 per month (Level-10 Pay Matrix).

<h2>About Cotton Corporation of India Limited</h2>

<h3>Company Overview</h3>

<p>Cotton Corporation of India Limited (CCI) is a government company under the Ministry of Textiles, engaged in India's cotton trade and procurement. The organization plays a vital role in supporting cotton farmers and the textile industry.</p>

<h3>Key Functions</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Procurement of cotton from farmers at fair prices</li>
  <li>Quality assessment and testing facilities</li>
  <li>Market price stabilization through interventions</li>
  <li>Export promotion of Indian cotton</li>
  <li>Research and development support for cotton sector</li>
  <li>Support to cotton growers and textile industry</li>
</ul>

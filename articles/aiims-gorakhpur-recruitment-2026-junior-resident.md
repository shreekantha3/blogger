<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>Q1. What is the duration of the Junior Resident post?</h3>
<p>A. The appointment is for <strong>6 months on ad-hoc basis</strong>. The post may be regularized based on future requirements and government policies.</p>

<h3>Q2. How many posts are available for Junior Resident?</h3>
<p>A. There are <strong>12 Junior Resident (Non-Academic) posts</strong> available for the walk-in interview.</p>

<h3>Q3. Do I need to have FMGE for this recruitment?</h3>
<p>A. Yes, all foreign medical graduates must have <strong>FMGE certificate</strong> along with valid registration with Medical Council of India.</p>

<h3>Q4. Is there any age relaxation for PwBD candidates?</h3>
<p>A. Yes, PwBD candidates are eligible for <strong>10-15 years</strong> additional relaxation depending on the disability category as per government rules.</p>

<h3>Q5. Can I apply without paying the application fee?</h3>
<p>A. PwBD candidates can apply <strong>without paying any fee</strong> as the application fee is completely waived off for persons with disabilities.</p>

<h3>Q6. What happens after the walk-in interview?</h3>
<p>A. Selected candidates will receive appointment letters and joining instructions through official communication. All selected candidates must complete medical fitness verification.</p>

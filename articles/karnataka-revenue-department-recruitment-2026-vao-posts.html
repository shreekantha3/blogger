Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Post Minimum Qualification
Village Administrative Officer Second PUC (Class 12) Examination by Karnataka Pre University Education Board OR equivalent qualification recognized by Government (CBSE/ICSE/Class 12 from other state boards, NIOS, ITI with language + general subject)

Age Limits (as of Recruitment Date)

  • General/Unreserved: 18-40 years
  • Category 2A/2B/3A/3B (Karnataka): 18-43 years
  • SC/ST/Cat-1 (Karnataka): 18-45 years
  • Minimum Age: 18 years (common for all categories)
  • Age Relaxation: As per Government rules for reserved categories

Important Rules

  • Candidates can apply for only one district - multiple district applications will not be accepted
  • Working knowledge of Kannada is essential
  • Knowledge of basic computer applications preferred

Salary Structure

Pay Scale Details

Selection Process

The recruitment selection process follows a multi-stage approach:

Step 1: Written Competitive Examination

  • Objective type questions on General Knowledge, Reasoning, and Kannada language
  • Offline-OMR based test

Step 2: Language Test

  • Kannada language proficiency assessment

Step 3: Document Verification

  • Verification of educational certificates
  • SSLC and PUC certificate validation

Step 4: Probation Period

  • Selected candidates join 2-year probation period
  • Pensionary benefits under National Pension Scheme (NPS)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for multiple districts?

No, candidates can apply for only one district. Multiple district applications will not be accepted.

What is the educational qualification for VAO?

Candidates must have passed 2nd PUC (Class 12) or equivalent from Karnataka PU Board or recognized boards (CBSE/ICSE/NIOS).

What happens after selection?

Selected candidates undergo a 2-year probation period with pensionary benefits under the National Pension Scheme (NPS).

Is this recruitment still ongoing?

The application deadline was July 30, 2026. Check the official website for any extended deadline or next recruitment cycle.

What is the salary for VAO?

The salary is ₹34,100 - ₹67,600 per month (one of the highest for Group-C positions in Karnataka).

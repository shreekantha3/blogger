Last Updated: July 2026 | Total Posts: 3 | Application Mode: Online

CSIR - Central Food Technological Research Institute (CFTRI), Mysuru has announced recruitment for 2026. 3 Project Associate-I positions are available under a prestigious research project. This is an excellent opportunity for candidates with a background in Zoology, Botany, Chemistry, or Biotechnology to join India's premier food research institute.

Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Essential Educational Qualifications

Candidates must possess either of the following qualifications:

  • M.Sc. in: Zoology, Botany, Chemistry, or Biotechnology from a recognized university
  • OR Bachelor's Degree in Engineering/Technology in: Mechanical or Chemical Engineering stream

Age Limit

  • Maximum Age: 35 years as on last date of application
  • Age Relaxation:
    • SC/ST/PwBD and Women candidates: 5 years
    • OBC candidates: 3 years

Important Conditions

  • Only candidates with declared final qualification results are eligible
  • Not eligible if you have already completed 6 years as CSIR Project Staff
  • The engagement is purely temporary and contractual
  • Initial tenure: up to 31 March 2027, extendable up to 31 March 2028 based on project requirements

Salary/Stipend

Qualification Status Monthly Stipend
With NET/GATE Qualification ₹31,000 per month + HRA
Without NET/GATE Qualification ₹25,000 per month + HRA

HRA will be provided as per CSIR norms applicable at the place of posting.

Job Responsibilities

Project Associate-I Role and Duties

  • Carry out laboratory experiments related to food safety and insect pest control
  • Assist in testing and validating multi-fume fumigation technology for food commodity storage
  • Record and analyze experimental data accurately
  • Prepare technical notes, reports, and documentation
  • Support senior scientists in day-to-day project activities
  • Maintain laboratory equipment and follow safety protocols
  • Coordinate with project team to meet research timelines

Desirable Qualification

  • Experience in research work related to food safety and protection
  • Knowledge of fumigation techniques and pest control methods

Required Documents Checklist

  • Experience Certificates (if applicable)
  • NET/GATE Certificate (if applicable)
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)

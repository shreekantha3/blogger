DFCCIL Recruitment 2026: 1 AGM/ JGM/ DGM Finance Post Apply Offline

Last Updated: July 2026 | Total Posts: 1 | Application Mode: Offline

Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Limited (DFCCIL) has announced recruitment for 1 senior Finance post for 2026. The AGM/JGM/DGM Finance position is available at the Corporate Office in Noida on deputation basis. This is a high-level opportunity for experienced government and PSU finance professionals.

Detailed Eligibility Criteria by Level

For AGM Level

  • Government Officers: Holding Level-13A analogous grade OR Level-13 with minimum 12+ years Group A service
  • PSU Officers: Working in E6 grade (₹90,000-2,40,000 IDA) with 4 years service

For JGM Level

  • Government Officers: Holding Level-13 analogous grade OR JAG Level-12 with 8-12 years Group A service
  • PSU Officers: Working in E5 grade (₹80,000-2,20,000 IDA) with 4 years service

For DGM Level

  • Government Officers: Holding Senior Scale Level-11 OR Group B with 10 years service in Level-10 post
  • PSU Officers: Working in E4 grade (₹70,000-2,00,000 IDA) with 4 years service

Age Limit

  • Maximum Age: 55 years (as of closing date)
  • Relaxation: As per Government of India/DFCCIL rules

Selection Process

Stage 1: Application Screening

How to Apply Offline

Step-by-Step Application Process:

  1. Download Application Form: Get the prescribed deputation proforma from DFCCIL official website under Careers/Jobs section
  2. Fill Application: Complete personal data, educational qualifications, experience details, and APAR ratings for last 5 years
  3. Obtain No Objection Certificate: Get NOC from parent department/organization
  4. Gather Required Documents:
  • Attested APAR ratings for last 5 years
  • Vigilance/DAR clearance certificate
  • Forwarding certificate from competent authority
  • Service, pay scale and experience details
  • Recent passport size photograph
  1. Proper Channel Forwarding: Must be forwarded through proper channel from your parent department/organization
  2. Envelope Superscription: "Application for the Post of AGM/JGM/DGM (Finance) against Vacancy Notice No. 24/2026"
  3. Send To: General Manager (HR), DFCCIL Corporate Office Complex, Sector-145, Noida, Uttar Pradesh - 201306

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the educational qualification required for DFCCIL AGM/JGM/DGM Finance post?

This is a deputation basis post requiring serving government officers or PSU employees in equivalent grades. Specific educational qualifications are detailed in the official notification.

How many vacancies are available for DFCCIL Finance recruitment?

There is 1 vacancy for AGM/JGM/DGM (Finance) position at the Corporate Office, Noida.

What is the maximum age limit for this post?

The maximum age limit is 55 years as of the closing date of application. Age relaxation is applicable as per standard rules.

Can fresh graduate apply for DFCCIL AGM/JGM/DGM post?

No. This is a deputation post open only to serving IRAS or CPSE officers who already hold an equivalent grade. Fresh graduates cannot apply.

What is the selection process for DFCCIL Finance recruitment?

The selection involves Application Screening, Document Verification (including vigilance/DAR clearance and APAR ratings), followed by Final Deputation Decision. There is no written exam.

How should I send my application?

Send the duly filled application through proper channel from your parent department to General Manager (HR), DFCCIL Corporate Office, Sector-145, Noida - 201306.

Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

  • Minimum Requirement: Passed PUC (Class 12) or equivalent qualification from Karnataka Pre-University Board

Age Limits (as of Application Date)

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 33 years (varies by category)
  • Age Relaxation: SC/ST get 5 years additional relaxation
  • Category-1/2A/2B: As per Karnataka government rules

Physical Standards Requirements

  • Height: Minimum 165 cm for General Category
  • Chest: Minimum 85 cm (with expansion 90 cm)
  • Weight: As per medical standards

Selection Process

The KSP Civil Police Constable recruitment follows multiple stages. The process is rigorous and comprehensive.

Step 1: Written Examination

  • Conducted by Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA)
  • Objective type questions on General Knowledge, Reasoning
  • Two sessions on August 2, 2026

Step 2: Physical Standard Test (PST)

  • Verification of physical measurements
  • Height, chest, and weight criteria check
  • Medical fitness examination

Step 3: Physical Endurance Test (PET)

  • Tests physical fitness and stamina
  • Includes running and physical activities

Step 4: Final Selection

  • Document verification
  • Final merit list preparation

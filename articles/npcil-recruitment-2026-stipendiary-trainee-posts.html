Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Available Disciplines

  • Plant Operator - For operating nuclear plant equipment
  • Electronics Mechanic - For electronic systems maintenance
  • Instrument Mechanic - For instrumentation systems
  • Electrician - For electrical maintenance
  • Fitter - For mechanical fitting work
  • Turner - For machining operations
  • Machinist - For precision machining
  • Welder - For welding and fabrication

Educational Qualification for Plant Operator

  • Minimum Requirement: HSC (10+2) or ISC with Science Stream (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)
  • Aggregate Marks: Minimum 50% marks in aggregate
  • English: Must have English as a subject at SSC (10th) level

Educational Qualification for Technical Trades

  • SSC (10th): Minimum 50% marks in Science and Mathematics individually
  • ITI Certificate: 2 years ITI certificate in relevant trade is mandatory
  • Experience (for shorter ITI): For trades with ITI duration less than 2 years, minimum 1 year relevant working experience required

Age Limit

  • Maximum Age: As per Government of India guidelines for NPCIL posts
  • Relaxation: Applicable as per standard rules for SC/ST/OBC/PwBD categories

Application Fee

Selection Process

Stage 1: Online Examination

  • Mode: Computer-based test (CBT)
  • Language: Trilingual question paper (Tamil, Hindi, English)
  • Structure: Two stages - Preliminary Test and Advanced Test
  • Qualification: Must qualify at each stage for final empanelment

Stage 2: Document Verification

  • Verification of educational certificates
  • Certificate validation for ITI and experience
  • Final merit list preparation

How to Apply Online

Step-by-Step Application Process:

  1. Visit Official Website: npcilcareers.co.in (Only this website is valid)
  2. Read Notification: Download and carefully read the official notification
  3. Check Eligibility: Verify educational and age criteria for your discipline
  4. Online Registration: Click on Apply → Online Registration link and provide basic information
  5. Activate Account: Check email and click activation link
  6. Fill Application: Login and complete Educational Qualifications, Work Experience, and Personal Details
  7. Upload Documents: Photo & Signature as per specifications
  8. Submit Application: Final submission before deadline

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the total number of vacancies in NPCIL Recruitment 2026?

There are 255 total vacancies for Stipendiary Trainee/Technician posts across SC, ST, OBC, EWS, and UR categories.

What are the educational qualifications required?

Plant Operator requires HSC with Science stream and 50% marks. Technical trades require SSC with 50% in Science and Mathematics plus 2 years ITI in relevant trade.

How can I apply for NPCIL recruitment?

Apply online at npcilcareers.co.in only. No other applications will be accepted. Follow the step-by-step registration process.

What is the application fee structure?

Male candidates from General/EWS/OBC categories pay ₹100. Female candidates and SC/ST/PwBD candidates are exempt from the application fee.

What is the selection process for NPCIL Stipendiary Trainee posts?

The selection is based on Online Examination (Preliminary + Advanced stages) followed by Document Verification. The exam is available in Tamil, Hindi, and English.

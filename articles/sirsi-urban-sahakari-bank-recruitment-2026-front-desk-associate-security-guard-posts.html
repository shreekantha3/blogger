Selection Process

The recruitment selection process follows a multi-stage approach:

Step 1: Offline Application

  • Download application form from bank website
  • Send completed application with documents to bank address

Step 2: Document Screening

  • Review of applications against eligibility criteria
  • Verification of educational qualifications

Step 3: Written Examination

  • Objective type questions on General Knowledge (50 marks)
  • Kannada Language Test (50 marks)
  • Total: 100 marks

Step 4: Interview

  • 15% weightage for interview after written exam
  • Assessing communication and suitability for role

Step 5: Document Verification

  • Final verification of original documents
  • Certificate validation

Step 6: Probation Period

  • Successful candidates undergo 6-month probation
  • Minimum basic pay during probation period

<h2>Karnataka Excise Recruitment FAQ</h2>

<h3>Q1. What is the total number of vacancies in Karnataka Excise Recruitment 2025?</h3>
<p>A. There are <strong>1,207 total vacancies</strong> - 942 Excise Constable posts and 265 Excise Sub-Inspector posts.</p>

<h3>Q2. Is there any application fee for these posts?</h3>
<p>A. No, there is <strong>no application fee</strong> required for applying to Karnataka Excise Department recruitment. The application process is completely free.</p>

<h3>Q3. Can I apply for both Constable and Sub-Inspector posts together?</h3>
<p>A. Yes, eligible candidates can apply for both categories if they meet the separate eligibility criteria for each post. However, you can only join one post if selected.</p>

<h3>Q4. What is the selection criteria for these posts?</h3>
<p>A. Selection is based on written examination, followed by Physical Efficiency Test (for Constables), Physical Standards Test (for Constables), and final document verification.</p>

<h3>Q5. What is the expected salary for Excise Constable?</h3>
<p>A. Excise Constable salary ranges from <strong>₹21,400 to ₹58,250</strong> per month under Level-1 Pay Matrix, along with allowances.</p>

<h3>Q6. How should I prepare for the Physical Efficiency Test?</h6>
<p>A. Start physical training 2-3 months in advance. Focus on running 1600m race, long jump, high jump, and shuttle run activities.</p>
